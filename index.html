Common Queries

Everything you need to know about date calculations and tax logic.

How are the nights calculated?
We use the standard hotel calculation method. If you select Dec 1st to Dec 8th, it is calculated as 7 nights. The check-out day is not counted as a billable night.
Is the 5% GST fixed for all amounts?
We currently apply a flat 5% rate which is standard for budget to mid-range hotels and restaurants in India. However, luxury hotels (tariff > ₹7,500) may attract 12% or 18% GST. This calculator is optimized for the standard 5% tier.
How are leap years handled?
Our algorithm is fully aware of calendar anomalies. If your date range includes February 29th of a leap year, it is correctly counted as a night in the total duration calculation.
